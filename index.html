Intro

Hi! Welcome to our lovely little site! We are a small team, of 4 people, Edmund, our CEO, Wayne our CDO and COO, Winson, our CTO, and isaiah, our CMO. We mainly code in swift/swiftui for ios/apple platforms, but we are currently learning kotlin for andriod mobile devleopment, and react/react native for web and multiplatform applications.

We are a non-profit organisation, creating apps for other non-profits, in order to solve issues that they have, and streamline their operations.

Work

We are currently working with TAVA.

Please reach out via the contact us page if you are a non-profit organisation looking for a mobile application

About

Founded in 2026, thegoodcompany is a small non-profit run by 4 students as part of SSTINC's project serve program, where students form small companies to build apps for free for non-profit organisations, we believe that in this new era in technology, everyone should have equal access to tools to improve their organisations efficiency and streamline their procceses.
